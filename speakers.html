TEDx SPEAKERS 2022

Mr. Puneeth Bekal

Mr. Puneeth Bekal is an experienced marketing professional with over 14 years of experience in managing large scale campaigns of huge titles in the industry like CEAT, Lodha, Godrej; in digital activation, media innovations, content strategy, etc. He is currently soaring as a director of marketing at Mastercard, and holds the distinction in consistently meeting and exceeding targets in a range of market subsectors.

Dr. Om Shrivastav

Dr. Om Shrivastav is a well-known infectious disease specialist. Dr. Om Shrivastav has been working as an Infectious Disease Physician for the past 17 years, gaining proficiency and sparse skills in his field. He earned his MBBS and MD in India and undertook further training in Sydney, Australia. Dr. Om is a multifaceted individual, he is a member of several review committees, a passionate teacher, an internationally invited orator and an exceptional author.

Mr. Suvrat Joshi

Suvrat Joshi is an Indian drama and television actor. He is widely known for his work in the Marathi sitcom, Dil Dosti Duniyadari and its sequel, Dil Dosti Dobara. He is an acting and music graduate from National School of Drama. Suvrat started off his career in theatre, before going into television. He also debuted as a singer in Dokyala Shot where he sang in Tamil. He hosted the dance reality show, Dance Maharashtra Dance. Suvrat produced and acted in his play Amar Photo Studio which rose to fame and received a lot of praise in the Marathi theatre community.

Mr. Kingshuk Chakravarty

Mr. Kingshuk is a film music director who predominantly works in Bollywood music. He has composed scores for a variety of movies including the 2020 movie “Suraj pe Mangal Bhari” and various trp topping TV shows. He has also headed Music Department for TV series “Bhram” and the 2019 released movie “The Zoya Factor”.

Mr. Rishabh Karwa

Rishabh Karwa is the Managing Director & Co-Founder at GoMechanic. He has been featured in Forbes 30 under 30 (India & Asia). Rishabh is an extremely versatile person, a keen-hearted man, a football aficionado, an astronomy enthusiast and these are only a few of the incredible feathers in his hat. Gomechanic’s vision of being reliable and cost effective has won encouragement and recognition across the globe. It has extended its wings to Malaysia in 2021, giving the country its first ever "Car servicing and maintenance via App".
